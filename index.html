The Mediterranean
SIDON · THE SEA WAS ALWAYS THERE
Visible from school. Visible from home.
Growing up in Sidon with the Mediterranean visible from your school window gives you something hard to explain. A sense of scale. A daily reminder that the world is bigger than whatever problem you're sitting with. The sea was always there. It still is.
GRADE 10 · PADI OPEN WATER · 20M
Twenty meters down. No noise.
No notifications. No noise. Just pressure, light, and your own breathing. You trust your equipment completely or you don't go down. There is no debug mode at depth. Every engineer should dive once — you learn what it means for a system to have zero margin for error.
"The sea taught me that calm is a skill, not a personality trait. You practice it until it becomes automatic. I learned that at 20 meters. I use it every day."
— AHMAD MIDO HARI · SIDON
